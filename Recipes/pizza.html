Pizza time!

Pizza! Everyones favourite (Or should be) dish, today I'm going to share with you a quick and simple recipe. This never fails me, wether it be an afterthought or in advance meal you can be sure that this will hit the mark everytime! This shall use pretty much standard pantry items, if not I'm sure you live close enough to a shop! So stop reading and get going!

  • 500g Strong White flour (Plus for dusting)
  • 7g Yeast
  • Pinch of salt
  • Teaspoon of sugar
  • 400ml Lukewarm water (might not need it all)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese and various toppings

Steps to creating your pizza

  1. Grab a bowl and place your ingredients except for the water, make sure they don't touch yet
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together, then slowly add the water while mixing
  3. While bringing this dough together, get it to a sticky consitency
  4. Dust some flour on the work surface, then start kneading!
  5. Grab your dish which you're using for the pizza and stretch it out and let it prove for 30minutes
  6. Grab your tomato sauce and smoother the top of the pizza and adorn with any toppings you see fit! (Maybe not pineapple)
